Adding Numbers

  • Paste numbers in the "Add Numbers" field (one per line)
  • Check "Add country code" if numbers don't have country codes
  • Select your country code from the dropdown
  • Click "Add to List" to move numbers to the final list
  • You can add multiple batches of numbers

Number Format Examples

Without country code:

501234567 507654321 553344556

With country code:

+966501234567 +966507654321 +966553344556

Sending Messages

  • Make sure WhatsApp Desktop is installed and logged in
  • Enter your message in the message field
  • Set your preferred interval (default is 3 seconds)
  • Click "Start Sending" to begin
  • Click "Always allow" when WhatsApp first opens
  • Manually click "Send" in WhatsApp for each contact

Tips

  • You can edit the final numbers list directly if needed
  • Use longer intervals for slower systems
  • Keep WhatsApp Desktop running during the process
  • Don't close the browser while sending
